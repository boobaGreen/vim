# Moving in the Void.

Forget the arrow keys. In Vim, your right hand should never leave the home row.

### The Four Pillars
Use these keys for every movement:

<DirectionalGrid />

<InfoBox type="tip" title="WHY hjkl?">
The `hjkl` keys date back to the ADM-3A terminal, but the real reason is efficiency: your fingers stay on the home row, saving milliseconds that add up to hours.
</InfoBox>

### The Maze Challenge
To proceed, you must move the cursor (green square) to the end of the line, represented by the `$` symbol.

<HJKLMaze />

<Card title="🧠 Mental Exercise">
  Imagine a 5x5 grid. The cursor is in the top-left and you need to reach the bottom-right.
  Which sequence is shorter?
  - `jjjjllll` (4 down + 4 right = 8 keys)
  - `ljljljlj` (alternating = 8 keys)
  - Both work, but over time you'll learn faster combos!
</Card>
