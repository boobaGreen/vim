# Moving in the Void.

Forget the arrow keys. In Vim, your right hand should never leave the home row.

### The Four Pillars
Use these keys for every movement:
- `h`: Left (Backward)
- `j`: Down (Towards the earth)
- `k`: Up (Towards the sky)
- `l`: Right (Forward)

### The Maze Challenge
To proceed, you must move the cursor (green square) to the end of the line, represented by the `$` symbol.
