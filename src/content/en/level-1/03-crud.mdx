# Create and Destroy.

In Vim, you don't just type. You manipulate text with precise gestures.

### Fundamental CRUD Commands
Here's how to interact with text in **Normal Mode**:

- `i`: **Insert** before the cursor. (Enters Insert Mode)
- `x`: **Delete** the character under the cursor.
- `u`: **Undo** the last action. (Vital!)
- `Ctrl + r`: **Redo** the undone action.

### Practical Practice
Move the cursor to the terminal on the right. Try deleting a word with `x` and then restoring it with `u`.

> [!TIP]
> Remember: to return to **Normal Mode** after using `i`, always press `<ESC>`.
