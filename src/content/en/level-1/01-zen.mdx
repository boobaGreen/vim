# Welcome to the Void.

Vim is not just a text editor; it is a **language for text manipulation**. Its power comes from a fundamental concept: **modality**.

### Why Modes?
In most editors, every key you press writes a character. In Vim, the keyboard is a control instrument.

- **Normal Mode**: The default state. Here, keys are commands.
- **Insert Mode**: Where you type text, like in a regular editor.
- **Visual Mode**: For selecting blocks of text.

> "In Vim, you don't write code. You carve it."
