# Need for Speed?

It's time to put your new grammar to the test. In this challenge, it's not just about *what* you type, but *how many* keys you press. In Vim, fewer keys mean more efficiency.

### The Rules
1. You will be given a **SOURCE** text.
2. You must transform it into the **TARGET** goal.
3. The system will count every single keystroke.

### Recommended Strategy
Use combinations like `dw`, `cw`, or counts like `d2w` to minimize your steps.
