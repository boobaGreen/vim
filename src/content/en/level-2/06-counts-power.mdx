# Multiplying the Action.

Vim is not just precise; it is scalable. By inserting a number (**Count**) before a command, you can repeat it as many times as you want without effort.

### The Full Formula
Remember the grammar? `[VERB] + [COUNT] + [MOTION]`

- `3w`: Jump forward 3 words.
- `d2w`: Delete the next 2 words.
- `c3e`: Change until the end of the 3rd following word.

### When to use Counts?
Counts are perfect when you see an obvious repetition. Instead of pressing `dw` three times, press `d3w`. Save keys, save time, reduce cognitive friction.
